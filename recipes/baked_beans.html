Clean and chop kale and dice potatoes (1/2 lb per person)

Season potatoes with salt, pepper and oil, lay out on baking sheet. Season chicken with salt pepper and oil, add to baking sheet. Roast together for 30min

Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings here

Slice yellow onion, and mince garlic (1 clove per person)

Add onion and garlic ot a medium sauce pot with 2 tablespoons of oil and salt, place over medium heat

Allow vegetables to soften, then add kale to the pot in batches until completely wilted

Add pinto beans, BBQ sauce and 1 cup of water to the pot, bring to a simmer and reduce to low heat

When the potatoes are finished cooking, remove from the sheet pan, turn the oven to 500 degrees and return the chicken to the oven for 5 min

Plate the chicken, potatoes and pinto beans, serve and enjoy!
