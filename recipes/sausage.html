Lightly rub sausages with cooking oil and place in an oven proof pan or sheet tray, roast for 20 min

Slice pepper, set aside

Slice onions, set aside

Clean and mince garlic cloves, set aside

De-rib, clean and chop kale, set aside

Preheat large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and add onions and pepper to the pan

Allow the vegetables to cook until they begin to soften and the onions begin to caramelize, then add the garlic to the pan and saute until fragrant

Add the kale to the pan and saute until the greens begin to wilt, season with salt

Add the beans to the pan, reduce heat to a simmer and cook until the greens are tender

When sausages have finished cooking, remove from oven and allow to rest for 5min. When veg is ready, remove from heat and adjust seasoning as necessary, plate the sausage over top of the vegetables and enjoy!
