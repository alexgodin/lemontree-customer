Ingredient

Preheat oven to 400 degrees and slice the onion, clean and chop the kale and mince the garlic, set aside

Dice the chicken, set aside

Preheat large saute pan over high heat, add 2 tablespoons of oil and the chicken to the pan

When the chicken is browned on one side, add the onion the pan and saute until tender

When the onion is ready, add the kale to the pan and saute until it begins to wilt

When the kale has cooked, add the garlic to the pan and half of the enchilada sauce, and reduce the sauce to a glaze

Warm the tortillas in a microwave for 30 seconds and set aside

Assemble the enchiladas in a baking dish with some sauce underneath of the enchiladas to prevent them from sticking

Top the enchiladas with the remaining sauce and bake for 10min

Serve and enjoy!
