Ingredients

Equipment

Cut the broccoli into florets and slice the onion, set aside

Preheat oven to 450 degrees, toss the broccoli with salt and oil, lay on a baking sheet and roast for 20min until cooked

Combine the sliced onion with the chicken, season with salt and pepper and mix to combine

Form a single patty from the onion and chicken mix and set aside

Preheat a saute pan over high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and the patty to the pan

Sear on one side, flip and continue cooking until the patty is cooked through

While the chicken is cooking, smear each side of the rye bread with mustard

When the chicken is finished, sandwich the patty with the cheese in between the bread and return to the pan, browning on each side

When the sandwich and broccoli have finished cooking, slice the sandwich in half, serve with the broccoli and enjoy!
