Equipment

Prep:

  • Bring a pot of water to a boil and season with salt

Slice onion, clean and chop the escarole, quarter the lemon and mince the garlic, set aside

Dice the chicken thigh, set aside

Preheat a large saute pan over high heat, add 2 tablespoons of oil and the diced chicken

When the chicken is browned on one side, add the sliced onion and season with salt

When the onion has softened add the escarole to the pan and saute until the greens have wilted

While the escarole is cooking, season boiling water with salt and cook the pasta until tender

When the escaole is cooked, add the garlic to the pan and saute until fragrant and remove from the heat

When the pasta is cooked, strain and add the noodles and the lemon juice to the pan, cook until the water has evaporated and the noodles are combined with the greens

Serve the pasta and enjoy!
