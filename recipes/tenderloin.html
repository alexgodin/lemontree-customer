Prep:

  • Preheat oven to 450Â°

Remove the pork from it's packaging and season with salt, pepper and any personal spices you may prefer, set aside

Dice the potato, set aside

Slice the cabbage, set aside

Slice the onion, set aside

Combine the potato, onion and cabbage in a mixing bowl, season with salt and cooking oil

Rub the pork tenderloin with cooking oil and place onto a sheet tray with the vegetables and set a timer for 16 minutes

Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings here.

Dice the apple, set aside

When the timer goes off, cut the pork in half and decide if it is done to your liking. If it's not, put the meat back in the oven until you are happy with it.

Once the vegetable have finished roasting, place in a mixing bowl, add the diced apple and season lightly with 1 tablespoon of vinegar

When the pork has finished cooking, remove from the oven and allow the meat to rest for 3-5 minutes before slicing. Slice the pork, serve on top of the roasted vegetables and enjoy!
