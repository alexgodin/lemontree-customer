Remove kernels from corn

Slice scallions (separating the white and greens sections), Slice cubanelle (1/2 per person), Dice celery, Mince garlic (2 cloves per person)

Preheat saute pan over medium heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and the pepper, celery, corn and scallion whites

Saute vegetables until lightly wilted, season with salt and add minced garlic to the pan

Saute garlic until fragrant and add the rice to the pan, toast the rice for 30 seconds

Add the tomato sauce, salt and 1/2cup of water to the pan, bring to a simmer, cover and reduce the heat to low. Cook the rice covered for 20min and allow to rest for 5 additional minutes off the heat

Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings here.

While the rice cooks preheat a small saute pan over medium heat, add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil and sear the sausages on both sides. Place the sausage in the oven to finish cooking for another 5min, remove from the oven and slice to serve.

Adjust the seasoning on the rice if need be, plate the rice and the sausage, garnish with the reserved scallion greens and enjoy!
