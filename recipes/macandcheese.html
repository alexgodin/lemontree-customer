Prep:

  • Bring a pot of water to a boil and season with salt

Cut top of broccoli and stem into bite size chunks (avoid any woody parts of the stem). Slice red onion (1/4 onion per person) and set aside

Preheat large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoons of oil and the broccoli florets to the pan

When broccoli begins to brown, add the sliced onion to the pan, season with salt and saute until the veg begins to soften

Add milk and 1/2 cup of water per person to the pan, bring to a simmer

Once simmering, add the cheddar and cream cheese, allow the cheeses to melt, season with black pepper and remove from the pan from the heat

Add the cavatappi to the boiling water and cook until tender

When cooked, drain the pasta and add to the pan, toss to combine with the sauce and adjust the seasoning as necessary

Allow the pasta to cook for 1min in the sauce, serve and enjoy!
