De-rib and chop collards, slice onion (about 1/2 per person), and mince garlic (1 clove per person), set aside

"

Preheat large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and the onions to the pan

Allow the onions to caramelize, then add the garlic and collards, season with salt and lightly saute

Add 2cups of water to the pan, bring to a simmer and cover the pan, cook for 15-20min

Once water is boiling use a whisk or fork to stir the water while adding the grits. Continue stirring until the grits begin to thicken. Reduce the heat to low, allow the grits to cook for 10min and season with salt to taste

When the wings have finished cooking, remove from the oven and toss with the hot sauce (optional: if someone in your family doesn't like spicy food, skip this step for their wings!)

Plate the wings with the grits and greens, serve and enjoy!
