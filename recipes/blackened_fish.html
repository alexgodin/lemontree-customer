Ingredients

Equipment

Clean and chop green beans, mince garlic and set aside

Season the tilapia filet with the spice packet, salt, pepper, and set aside

Bring 1/2cup of water to a boil, season lightly with salt and oil, add the cous cous, cover and remove from the heat allowing to stand for 5 min

While the cous cous is cooking, preheat a saute pan over high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and add one tilapia filet to the pan. Allow the fish to blacken slightly on one side, then flip and finish cooking the fish until fully cooked and remove from the pan. Repeat process for each filet.

In the same pan, add 1 tablespoon of additional cooking oil and the green beans, saute until the beans begin to soften

When the beans are ready, add the garlic and continue sauteing until fragrant and remove from the heat

When finished, plate the fish and the green beans over top of the cous cous and enjoy!
