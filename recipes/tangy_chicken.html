Slice half a red onion per person, set aside

Slice one red pepper per person, set aside

Lay red onion and bell pepper in a baking dish, add marinated chicken and marinating liquid, roast for 25-30 minutes until cooked through

Chop cucumber, season lightly with salt and white sugar, set aside

When rice is finished, add marinated cucumbers and toss to combine. When chicken thighs have finished cooking, serve roast chicken and vegetables over top of the rice and enjoy!
