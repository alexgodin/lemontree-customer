Equipment

Prep:

  • Bring 3/4 cups of water to boil per person

Dice the pepper, and set aside

Slice zucchini, set aside

Mince the garlic, set aside

Slice the onion and set aside

Season ground beef with salt and pepper

Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings here.

Preheat a large pan over medium/high heat, add ground beef and sear allowing the beef to brown as much as possible.

Once the beef is browned, add the onion and pepper to the pan and saute until vegetables begin to soften

When ready add the minced garlic, saute until fragrant

Add the diced tomatoes and the zuccini to the pot and bring the mixture to a simmer

Continue cooking the tomato sauce, making sure to stir regularly, until the liquid has reduced by half

Once the water is at a boil, season with salt, add 1 tablespoon of oil and your couscous. Cover the pot and remove from the heat.

The couscous will take around 5min to cook. When finished set aside to rest.

Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings here.

When the vegetables have finished cooking, serve the stew over top of the couscous and enjoy!
