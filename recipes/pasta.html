Preheat your oven to 450 degrees and bring a pot of water to a boil

Remove your ground chicken from the package and season with salt and any other spices you might prefer while mixing lightly

Cut the brocolli crowns into florets, and dice the broccoli stem to a similar size

Clean and slice the red pepper into strips

In a mixing bowl, toss the cut broccoli and red pepper in salt and 2 tablespoons of oil, and lay out on your baking sheet

Clean and slice 1 onion, set aside

Peal and mince 4 cloves of Garlic, set aside

Place the tray of cut broccoli in your oven for around 20min or until the broccoli is roasted

Heat a pan over medium-high heat and add 2 tablespoons of oil, add your seasoned chicken in four separate patties

Sear each side of the chicken patties until browned, and then break up the meat with a rubber spatula or wooden spoon

Add your sliced onion, season with salt and add additional cooking oil if the pan appears to be dry

Once the onion has softened, add your minced garlic to the pan and saute until fragrant

Add your tomato paste to the pan and saute until thoroughly mixed throughout the meat and vegetables

When ready, add the roasted vegetables to your pan and cover with 1 cup of water

Season your boiling water with salt to taste and add the dried pasta, stirring occasionaly to ensure the pasta does not stick

While your pasta is finishing, continue cooking your chicken and vegetable sauce until it begins to thicken

Once the pasta is cooked, drain out the water through a colander and return the pasta to your pot.

Combine your pasta and your sauce, cooking gently over medium heat until no water remains and the penne is coated with the sauce

If you prefer, you can now add parmesean cheese, serve and enjoy!
