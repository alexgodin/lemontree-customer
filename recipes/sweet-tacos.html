Slice plantain, toss with oil and salt, lay out on a baking sheet and roast for 20min until caramelized

Slice onion, separate into two and marinate half with vinegar, oil and salt to taste, and set the rest aside

Dice tomatillo, remove corn kernels (1/2 ear per person) and mince garlic (1 clove per person)

Preheat large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and add the beef to the pan

Allow the beef to lightly brown and then add the reserved onion, corn and tomatillo to the pan, and season with salt

Allow the vegetables to soften and add the garlic, saute until fragrant

Add 1 cup of water per person to the pan and ring the liquid to a simmer and reduce heat to low, allow the mixture to braise until the beef is tender, the tomatillos are broken down and and water has mostly evaporated

Heat the tortillas from 1min in the microwave and assemble the tacos with the braised beef, pickled onions and roasted plantains
