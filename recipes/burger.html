Cut sweet potato into wedges, toss with salt and oil, set aside

Dice onion and mince garlic, set aside

Lay out the sweet potato on a baking sheet, roast for 20min until golden brown and tender

Combine the onion, garlic and ground beef in a mixing bowl, fold the vegetables into the meat with a rubber spatula and form into patties

Preheat large saute pan over medium-high heat, add the patties to the pan and cook on each side for 3-5min until cooked to desired internal temperature. When the patties have finished cooking remove from the heat, slice the pepper and add to the pan

Saute the pepper in the rendered fat from the patties and season with salt to taste, remove from the heat when tender

Assemble the burgers with the sauted peppers over the top and the sweet potato fries on the side, serve and enjoy!
