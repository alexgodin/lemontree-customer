Prep:

  • Preheat oven to 450Â°

Cut Sweet Potato into fries, set aside

Cut Zucchini set aside

Slice 1/2 small onion per person, set aside. For large onions, slice about 1/4 onion per person. (save a handful of sliced onions for later)

Slice pepper, set aside

Toss vegetables in 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and salt, toss to combine and lay out on sheet tray, roast for 20min

Mince Garlic

Combine ground beef with garlic and extra sliced onion, season with salt and pepper, form into patties and set aside

Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings here.

When the vegetables have 5 minutes left in the oven, preheat a large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil. Add the beef patties and sear on each side

If the saute pan is oven safe you can finish the patties in the oven, roasting for 2-3 minutes, or transfer the patties to the baking sheet and finish in the oven if your pan is not heat safe.

Adust the seasoning of the vegetables when finished cooking and allow the burgers to rest for 3 minutes

Serve the patties on the potato rolls and add any condiments you prefer
