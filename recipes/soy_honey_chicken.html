Equipment

Slice the pepper and onion, cut the broccoli and mince the garlic, set aside

Bring 1cup of water to a boil, season with salt, add the rice, cover, reduce heat to low and cook for 20min

Dice the chicken thigh, marinate with the soy sauce, garlic and honey, and season with salt and pepper

Preheat large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil, add the broccoli to the pan and allow to caramelize

Add the onion, carrot and peppers to the pan, season with salt and saute until vegetables begin to soften

Add the marinating chicken to the pan with the marinating liquid and saute until the chicken is cooked through

Plate the steamed rice, serve the stir fry over the top and enjoy!
