Prep:

  • Preheat oven to 400Â°

Mince carrot, onion and garlic, set aside

Combine ground pork (note: this recipe uses twice as much pork as your other recipes) with the onion, carrot, half of the garlic, ketchup, mustard and egg

Form into a loaf and place on a baking sheet

Dice the potato and season with oil and salt

Lay out the potatoes on the tray with the loaves and bake for 25min

Clean the green beans, set aside with the other half of the garlic

While the meatloaf bakes, preheat a saute pan over medium heat, add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil and the green beans to the pan

When the green beans begin to soften, add the garlic to the pan, season with salt and saute until fragrant

Add 2 tablespoons of water to the pan and allow the green beans to steam through until tender

When the meat loaf is finished cooking, remove from the oven, slice and serve alongside the greens beans and roasted potatoes
