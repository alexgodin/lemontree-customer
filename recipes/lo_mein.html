Prep:

  • Bring a pot of water to a boil and season with salt

Slice the carrots and red onions, chop the green beans, slice the zucchini and mince the ginger, set aside

Preheat the large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil per person and the ground chicken

Allow the ground chicken to brown on one side, season with salt and pepper and add the onion, carrot and green beans to the pan

While the vegetables are cooking, add the spaghetti to the boiling water and cook until tender

Once the vegetables are starting to soften, add the ginger and zucchini to the pan and saute until fragrant

Add the soy sauce and 1/4 cup of water per person to the pan and bring to a simmer

Once the spaghetti is finished cooking, strain the noodles and add to the pan, continue cooking until the sauce reduces and glazes the noodles

Adjust the seasoning if necessary, serve and enjoy!
