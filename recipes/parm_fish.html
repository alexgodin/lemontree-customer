Equipment

Slice the yellow onion, mince the garlic, clean and chop the kale and set aside

Season the tilapia filet with salt, pepper, then top with the parmesan cheese, press the cheese gently into the filet and set aside. Note: if you don't plan on using a non-stick skillet or would like for the recipe to be simpler, top with salt and pepper and save parmesan.

Bring 1cup of water to a boil, season lightly with salt and oil, add the rice, cover and cook for 20min over low heat

While the rice is cooking, preheat a saute pan over high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and add the onion to the pan

When the onions have begun to caramelize, add the kale and garlic to the pan and saute until the greens have begun to wilt

When cooked, remove the greens from the pan, wipe clean and return to the stove over medium heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and the tilapia, cheese side down

Allow the fish to cook gently on the cheese side, making sure not to allow the parmesan to burn, and when the cheese has become crisp you may flip the fish and finish cooking on the other side

When finished, plate the tilapia over top of the kale and rice, and enjoy! If you saved your cheese, now is a good time to top your dishes.
