Equipment

Bring 1 cup of water per person to a boil, add salt and the rice, cover and reduce heat to low, cook for 20min

Add soy sauce and black pepper to the beef, allow to marinate and set aside

remove stringy tip and chop green beans, slice carrots, mince garlic and ginger and slice scallions (separating white and green)

Preheat large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoons of oil and the green beans and carrots

Saute vegetables until they begin to soften and add garlic, ginger and scallion whites

Saute until fragrant and then add roughly 1/8 cup of water per person to the pan

Continue cooking until the water has evaporated, then add the beef and soy sauce to the pan. Cook the beef briefly (1-2min) until just cooked through but not dry, remove pan for the the heat and set aside

When rice has finished cooking, serve alongside the stir fry and enjoy!
