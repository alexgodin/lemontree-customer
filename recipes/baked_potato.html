Prick the potatoes (1 lb per person) with a fork and season potatoes with oil and salt, and microwave on high for 5min

Place the potatoes on a baking sheet and roast for 20min

Slice scallions (1 per person), separate the whites and the greens, mince garlic (1 clove per person), remove the corn kernels (1/2 per person)

Preheat large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoons of oil and the ground beef

Allow the beef to brown lightly, add the scallion whites and corn to the pan and season with salt

Allow vegetables to soften, add approx 1.5 tablespoons of water per person and allow to cook for 1 minute and remove from the heat when the mixture is incorporated

Preheat a small saute pan over medium heat, add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil and add the green beans to the pan

Lightly saute the beans for around 1min and then add the minced garlic and salt

Saute the garlic until fragrant and add 1/4cup of water to the pan per person, continue cooking until water has evaporated

When the potatoes have finished cooking, split open and divide the beef filling between the potatoes. Top the potatoes with the shredded cheese and return to the oven for 2min until the cheese has melted

Serve the stuffed potatoes with the sauted green beans and enjoy!
