Ingredients

Equipment

Clean and slice the cabbage, set aside

Peel ginger with a spoon, set aside.

Clean and mince the ginger, set aside

Clean and slice the scallion, set aside

Season the ground pork with salt, pepper and any other personal spices. Form into 4 small patties

Bring 2 cups of water to a boil, season with salt, add the rice, cover and reduce to low heat. Cook for 20 min.

Preheat and large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil and sear each side of the pork patties

With a wooden spoon, break up the patties into smaller pieces and add the minced ginger to the pan, saute until fragrant

Remove the ground pork from the pan and set aside in mixing bowl. Add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil to the pan and add the sliced cabbage

Saute the cabbage until softened and beginning to caramelize, remove from the pan and add to the bowl with the reserved pork

Add the green beans to the pan and saute over medium heat until tender

When the green beans have softened return the cabbage and pork to the pan, toss to combine, add the sliced scallions and soy sauce

Taste the stir-fry and adjust the seasoning as needed, fluff the rice with a fork, plate the stir-fry over top of the rice and enjoy!
