Slice cubanelle pepper, set aside

Slice scallions, separating the white and green parts

Mince the garlic cloves, set aside

Pre-heat a large saute pan over medium-high heat, crumble the beef in the pan and allow it to brown on one side

Season the beef with salt and pepper, and add the pepper and scallion whites to the pan

Once the pepper has softened, then add the minced garlic

Saute until fragrant, then add the can of black beans to the pan, bring to a simmer and reduce the liquid until almost completely dry

Build your quesadillas by add 1/4 of the bean mixture inside of each of the tortillas along with half the cheese

Fold and top with the remaining shreaded cheese and bake for 8--10 minutes

When finished, top with the scallion greens and your favorite condiments
