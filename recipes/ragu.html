Equipment

Prep:

  • Bring a pot of water to a boil and season with salt

Slice onion, set aside

Slice carrot, set aside

Mince garlic, set aside

Pre-heat a large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and crumble the ground beef into the pan

Season the meat with salt and pepper, break up into smaller pieces with a wooden spoon and add the sliced onion and carrot

Once the vegetables begin to soften, add the garlic and saute until fragrant

Add the crushed tomato to the pan, reduce heat to medium-low and allow the sauce to simmer. While the sauce simmers, add the dry pasta to your water and cook until tender.

When the pasta is finished, drain the cooking water, adjust the seasoning of your sauce if needed and toss the pasta in your beef ragu (if available, you can add parmesean cheese.) Serve and enjoy!
