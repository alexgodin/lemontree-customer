Slice onion, and cut potatoes and carrots, toss together with 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and salt. Roast vegetables for 20min until tender and well browned.

Slice 1/8 head cabbage per person and half apple per person (save half of your sliced apple), toss with salt, sugar, 1 tablespoon of vinegar and 2 tablespoons of oil, set aside

Prepare the sandwiches by slicing 1/2 of the block of cheddar, layering 1/4 of the cheese and 1/4 of an apple in between two slices of bread

Pre-heat large saute pan over medium heat, add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil and cook the sandwich on both sides until golden brown and the cheese is melted

Adjust the seasoning of the roasted vegetables when they have finished cooking

Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings here.

Plate the sandwich, cabbage salad and roasted vegetables, and enjoy!
