Ingredient

Equipment

Remove 1/4 lb ground chicken per person from the packaging and season with salt and pepper, form into 4 separate patties and set aside

Clean and slice 1/2 yellow onion per person, set aside

Clean and mince garlic, set aside

Clean and slice carrot, set aside

Clean and chop 1/4 head escarole per person, set aside

Preheat a large pot over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and sear chicken patties on both side until golden brown

When ready, break up the chicken patties into smaller pieces with a wooden spoon and add the onion and carrots to the pot

When the vegetables have begun to soften, add the minced garlic and escarole to the pot, saute until fragrant

Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings here.

Add the chopped tomatoes to the pot, bring to a simmer and cook until the liguid has reduced by 3/4

Add the cannellini beans to the pot with 2 cups of water, bring the stew to a simmer and allow to cook until liquid has reduced by half
