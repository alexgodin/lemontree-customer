Ingredient

Equipment

Season the ground beef with salt and pepper, and mix lightly

Clean and slice the onion, set aside

Clean and slice the carrot, set aside

Clean and mince the garlic, set aside

Clean the corn and remove the kernels, set aside

Preheat the large pot over medium-high heat, form patties with the beef and sear on each side

Break up the beef with a wooden spoon, and add the onion, carrot, corn and garlic

Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings here.

Add the tomato sauce to the pot, bring to a simmer and reduce by 3/4 until almost dry

Add the black beans to the pot and use the can to add additional water, bring to a simmer and allow to cook for 20min

Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings here.
