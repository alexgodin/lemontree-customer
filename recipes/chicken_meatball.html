Prep:

  • Bring a pot of water to a boil and season with salt

Dice onion and mince the garlic, and set aside

Mix the onion and garlic with the ground chicken, season with salt and pepper

Form into 5 meatballs per person and add to a large saute pan over medium-high heat, with 1 tablespoons of cooking oil per person

Sear the meatballs on one side and then flip over to sear on the opposite side as well

While the meatballs cook, cut the brocollli into florets and set aside

When both sides of the meatballs have been seared, add the broccoli to the pan, season with salt and allow the broccoli to cook until lightly softened

When ready, add the tomato paste to the pan, saute until mixed throughout the broccoli and meatballs

Add 1/2 cup milk per person and 1/2 cup of water per person and bring the mixture to a simmer, reduce heat to medium and allow the sauce to braise

While the meatballs braise, cook the penne pasta until tender, strain and add to the sauce. Reduce until sauce coats the noodles. Serve and enjoy!
