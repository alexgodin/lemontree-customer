Ingredients

Cut the potato and carrot and peel the garlic. Slice half of the onion into slivers and dice the other half into small pieces. Set aside

Cover the potato and garlic with water in a small pot, bring to a boil and reduce heat to medium

Cook the potatoes until tender, season the water with salt and add the tilapia fillets to the water

Continue cooking until the fish is cooked through and strain the potato and tilapia through a colander

Combine the potatoes, garlic and fish with the diced onion in a mixing bowl. Mash with a fork until well blended and place in the refrigerator until cool

Place the sliced onion and carrots in a separate mixing bowl, add the ranch dressing and set aside

When the potato and fish mixture is cool, form into 4 small patties per person. Return to the fridge

Preheat a large saute pan over high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil to the pan and cook the patties 4 at a time, searing on each side

When the patties are finished cooking, toss the chopped lettuce with the sliced onions, carrots and salad dressing. Serve the patties alongside the salad and enjoy!
