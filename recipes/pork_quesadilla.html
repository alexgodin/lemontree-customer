Prep:

  • Preheat oven to 350Â°

Slice onion and carrot, set aside

Preheat saute pan over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoon of cooking oil and the ground pork to the pan

Once the pork has browned on one side, add the carrot and onion, season with salt and saute vegetables

Once the vegetables have begun to soften, add the diced tomatoes to the pan and cook until the water has evaporated

Add the pinto beans to the pan, bring to a simmer and reduce until the liquid glazes the meat and vegetables

Adjust the seasoning if needed, assemble the quesadillas with the bean mixture and cheddar cheese, and bake for 8min

Remove the quesadillas from the oven, top with the remainder of the cheese and continue baking for 2min

Slice the quesadillas, serve and enjoy!
