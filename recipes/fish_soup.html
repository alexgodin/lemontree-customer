Ingredient

Equipment

Slice the yellow onion and celery, mince the garlic, dice the potato and set aside

Dice the tilapia and set aside

Preheat the pot over medium heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and the celery, onion and galic to the pan

Saute the vegetables gently until tender, then add the tomato sauce to the pan, bring to a simmer and cook until the liquid reduces by half

When ready, add the potatoes to the pot along with two cups of water per person and season with salt, bring to a simmer and cook until the potatoes are tender

When the potatoes are easy to pierce with a fork, add the tilapia to the stew and simmer very gently until the fish is just cooked through

Adjust the seasoning as necessary, plate the stew all together, top with the mayonnaise and enjoy!
