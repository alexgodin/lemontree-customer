Prep:

  • Bring a pot of water to a boil and season with salt

Dice the eggplant, slice the onion (1/2 per person) and mince the garlic (1 clove per person)

Preheat a large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoon of cooking oil and the eggplant, saute until vegetables begin to brown

Remove eggplant from the pan, add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil and the ground pork to the pan, saute until it begins to brown

Season the pork with salt, add onion and garlic, and saute until vegetables have begun to soften

Return the eggplant to the pan and add the tomato paste, saute until the paste is distributed and begins to caramelize

Add 1 cup of water to the pan per person and allow the mixture to braise, adjust the seasoning as necessary

While the water is reducing, cook the spaghetti until tender, strain through a colander and add to the pan sauce. Allow the pasta to coninue cooking until the sauce reduces to a glaze and coats the noodles.

Serve and enjoy!
