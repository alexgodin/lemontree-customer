Slice the Potatoes width wise into 1/4" coins and set aside

Slice the onion, set aside

Slice peppers, set aside

Remove the kernels from the corn cob, set aside

Mince the garlic, set aside

Crack the eggs into a mixing bowl, season with salt and pepper, and beat with a fork until combined

Place the potatoes, 2 Tablespoons of cooking oil and salt in a large saute pan, turn the heat to medium and saute until the potatoes begin to soften

Once the potatoes soften, add the onion and peppers, saute until the vegetables begin to soften

Add the corn and garlic, saute until fragrant

If your saute pan is oven safe, you may add the eggs directly to the pan and place the pan in the oven for 15-20 minutes or until the eggs are fully cooked. If your saute pan is not oven safe, you may place the vegetables in an oiled baking dish, add the eggs and stir to combine, and bake for 20-25 minutes

Once finished cooking, remove the frittata from the oven, allow to cool slightly, and slice into wedges
