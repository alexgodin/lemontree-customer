Ingredient

Dice onion, mince garlic, slice the pepper and remove the corn kernels from the cob, set aside

Slice the sausages in half lengthwise and set aside

Preheat a large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 1 tablespoon of oil to the pan and the sausages cut side down

When browned on the cut side, flip over and continue browning on the other side until the sausages are cooked through and remove from the pan

Add the corn, onion and peppers to the pan with the rendered sausage fat and cook until the vegetables begin to soften

Add the garlic to the pan and saute until fragratn, season to taste and remove from the heat

Warm the tortillas in a microwave for 30 seconds or in a pan separately. Assemble the tacos with half a sausage link in each one and the vegetables over the top, serve and enjoy!
