Slice the onion (1/2 per person) and pepper (1/2 per person) into strips, cut potato into wedges (one per person), peel corn (one per person) and break in half set aside. Set aside 1/2 of the pork you recieved.

Cut the potatoes into wedges, toss with salt, pepper and oil

Lay potatoes out on a baking sheet and roast for 10min. Toss corn with salt pepper and oil in the same bowl you used for the potatoes. When the 10min timer goes off for the potatoes, add the corn cobs to the pan with the potatoes and continue roasting for 10 more minutes

Form the pork sausage into 2 round patties, set aside

Preheat saute pan over medium heat, add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil and add the sausage patties to the pan

When pork is brown on each side (2-3min) add the add the onion and peppers to the pan. Don't stir the onion and peppers too much (it makes browning harder)

When the onion and pepper begin to brown, add 1/4cup of water to the pan and allow the pork sausage to finish cooking. Continue cooking until the water has evaporated and remove the pan from the heat

When the roasting vegetables are tender, remove the pan from the oven. When the water has evaporated, assemble the sandwiches with the pork patties onions and peppers. Serve the sandwiches with the roasted potatoes and corn, and enjoy!
