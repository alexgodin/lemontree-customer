--- title: Vegetarian Bean Tacos steps_dir: tacos redirect_from: /tacos layout: recipe-base ---

Ingredient

Slice red onion, season with salt and 1 tablespoon of prefered vinegar, set aside

Dice sweet potato, set aside

Cut corn kernels off the cob, set aside

Mince garlic cloves, set aside

Pre-heat large saute pan over medium-high heat, add cooking oil and sweet potato, saute until potato begins to brown and season with salt

Add corn to the pan, saute for 1 minute and add garlic to the pan and saute until fragrant

Add black beans to the pan and 1/4cup of salsa, bring to a simmer and allow vegetables to braise until potatoes are tender

When vegetables are tender, heat tortillas in the microwave for 30 seconds until soft

Assemble the tacos with potato/black bean mixture and top with onions and salsa.
