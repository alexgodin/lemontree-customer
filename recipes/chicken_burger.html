Ingredients

Dice sweet potato (1/2 per person), slice onion (1/4 per person) and mince garlic (1 clove per person)

Heat a pan with 1 tbsp oil over medium heat. Add the sweet potato.

While the potato cooks, combine the ground chicken, onion and garlic in a bowl, season with salt and pepper and mix to comibine

When potatoes begin to brown add half of the red onion. Cook until onion and potatoes are soft.

Form burger into patties. Preheat large saute pan over medium heat, add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil and add patties to the pan. While the burgers cook, slice the avocado and set aside. When the first side of the burgers are browned, flip and top with a sprinkle of shredded cheese (you may not use all of your cheese if you have multiple recipes that call for cheese), turn heat to low and allow the burgers to cook through fully

When the burgers have finished cooking remove from the pan, assemble the sandwiches with mayonnaise on the bun and top with the avocado
