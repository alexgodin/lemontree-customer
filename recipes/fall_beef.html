Prep:

  • Preheat oven to 450Â°

Mince one clove of garlic per person, combine with the beef, 1 tablespoon of oil per person, salt and pepper, and set aside

Cut the potatoes (1/2 lb per person) and carrots (1 per person) into wedges, and clean and halve the brussels sprouts, set aside

Season the potatoes and carrots with salt and oil, lay out on a baking sheet and roast for 20min

Preheat the saute pan over medium heat, add 1 tablespoons of oil per person to the pan and add the brussels cut side down

Roast until caramelized and tender, season with salt and remove from the heat

When the potatoes and carrots are finished cooking, remove from the oven and set aside

Once the vegetables are ready, clean out the saute pan, preheat over high heat, add 1 tablespoon of oil and the marinating beef, saute until cooked to desired doneness

When the beef has finished cooking, serve alongside the roasted vegetables and enjoy!
