Ingredients

Equipment

Dice the pork tenderloin into 1" cubes, marinade with salt, pepper, 1/2 of the yogurt and 1 tablespoon of oil, mix well and set aside

Dice the cucumber and slice the tomato, marinade with salt, pepper, remaining yogurt, 1 tablespoon of oil and vinegar to taste, set aside

Slice the onion, set aside

Slice the cabbage, set aside

Pre-heat large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and the sliced onion, saute until golden brown

Add the sliced cabbage to the pan, continue cooking until the cabbage is wilted and begins to brown

Remove vegetables from the pan and set aside, increase the heat to high and add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil, add the marinated pork to the pan

Do not stir the pork until the meat as browned nicely on one side, then saute the pork cooking on each side for around 30sec. Be careful of overcooking the pork. This process should be very quick.

Once the pork is cooked through, plate and serve with the cabbage and cucumbers.
