Season chicken quarter with salt, pepper and cooking oil. Lay out on a baking sheet and roast for 10min.

Cut broccoli into florets and season with salt, pepper, and cooking oil. Set aside.

Slice carrot and onion and mince garlic. Set aside.

When the chicken has baked for 10min, remove from the oven, add the broccoli to the pan, turn the heat down to 400 degrees and continue roasting for 15min.

Preheat a large saute pan over medium-high heat. Add the onion and carrots to the pan with 1/2 tablespoon of cooking oil per person and saute until vegetables begin to soften.

Add the garlic and rice to the pan, and saute until the garlic is fragrant.

Add the tomato sauce and 1/4cup of water to the pan and season with salt. Bring mixture to a simmer, cover and reduce heat to low. Cook for 20min.

When the chicken is cooked, removed from the oven and set aside to rest.

When the rice and chicken are cooked, adjust the seasoning if necessary, serve the chicken and broccoli over the rice and enjoy!
