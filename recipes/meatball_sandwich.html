Cut the sweet potato into wedges, toss with salt and oil, lay out on a baking sheet and roast for 20min. (If you don't want to use your oven, dice sweet potatoes and add to frying pan with salt, pepper and oil over medium heat until soft)

Dice the cubanelle pepper (1/2 per person) and mince the garlic (1 per person), set aside

Combine the pepper, garlic and ground turkey in a mixing bowl, season with salt, mix to combine

Form 4 small meatballs per person

Preheat a saute pan over medium heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and sear the meatballs on two sides

Add the tomato sauce to the pan, season with salt and bring to a simmer. Allow the meatballs to braise for 10min until cooked through, remove from the heat and allow to cool slightly

Cut the meatballs in half, retoss with the tomato sauce and assemble open-faced meatball sandwiches

Top the sandwiches with cheese, serve with the potatoes and enjoy!
