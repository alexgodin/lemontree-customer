Ingredients

Equipment

Note: Due to a product recall, we have changed this recipe to use Kale instead of romaine. To complete the substitution, just remove the stems from the kale and replace the romaine with Kale.

Thinly slice red onion

Place onion in a bowl and cover with vinegar and a few pinches of salt

Slice romaine hearts into salad size chunks

Optional: use a vegetable peeler to peel alternating strips of your cucumber

Slice cucumber into salad slices

Slice chicken into strips

Season chicken with salt, pepper and whatever other seasonings you have lying around

Preheat a lightly oiled pan over high heat. Cook chicken until brown on all sides (6-8 minutes). While chicken cooks, mix salad greens, cucumbers and pickled onions with salad dressing in a bowl

When chicken is done, slice into cubes and top the salad. Enjoy!
