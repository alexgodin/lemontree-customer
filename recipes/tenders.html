Prep:

  • Preheat oven to 450Â°

Cut carrot and sweet potato into french fry sized strips

Trim green beans and remove stringy ends

Cut chicken thighs into long strips. Set aside.

Toss carrots, sweet potatoes and green beans with salt, pepper, oil and any other spices you have on hand

Put the carrots, sweet potatoes and green beans on a baking sheet

Combine the mayonnaise and Dijon in a mixing bowl.

Combine the couscous and grated parmesan in another mixing bowl.

Coat chicken strips in mayonnaise mixture, then coat in couscous mixture. Put on another baking sheet.

Bake chicken and veggies for 10 minutes then remove from oven and flip. After flipping, return the baking sheets to the oven and turn down heat to 400 degrees. Bake for another 10-15 minutes until golden brown.

Plate the chicken fingers and veggie fries and enjoy!
