Equipment

Season chicken thighs with salt, add 1 tablespoon of oil to a pan, place chicken skin side down and turn heat to medium

Slice the onion, chop the carrots and celery, mince the garlic.

When the fat has rendered and the chicken skin is brown, drain off some of the rendered fat, turn the thighs and add the onion, carrot and celery to the pan, season with salt and saute until beginning to soften

When vegetables begin to soften, add the garlic and chopped tomatoes to the pan

Cook the liquid out of the tomatoes and add the beans and 1/2 cup of water per person to the pan.

Bring mixture to a simmer and then reduce heat to medium-low, continue cooking on low until chicken is tender

Adjust seasoning as needed, serve and enjoy!
