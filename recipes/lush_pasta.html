Equipment

Prep:

  • Bring a pot of water to a boil and season with salt

Remove ribs and chop kale

Slice red pepper

Mince garlic

Preheat large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil per person and ground turkey, allow to brown lightly

When turkey is brown add the red pepper to the pan

Saute pepper lightly, then add kale to the pan in batches until it has wilted

Add the garlic and tomato paste to the pan. When fragrant, Add 1 cup water per person to the pan, bring to a simmer and allow the sauce to braise until kale is tender

While kale cooks, add the penne to the salted water and cook until tender

When pasta has finished cooking, strain the cooking liquid and add the noodles to the sauce

Continue cooking the pasta with the sauce until the water has evaporated and the noodles are glazed

Serve and enjoy!

Optional: top with a bit of parmasean cheese if you have it lying around.
