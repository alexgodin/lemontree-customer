Ingredient

Slice red onion (1/2 per person), Mince garlic (1 clove per person), Remove corn kernels (1/2 ear per person) set aside

Preheat large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil and the ground pork to the pan. Allow the pork to brown and then break up into smaller pieces with a spoon or spatula

Add the onion and corn to the pan and continue to cook until the vegetables begin to soften

Add the garlic to the pan and saute until fragrant

Add black beans and 1/2 of the enchilada sauce to the pan, bring mixture to a simmer and reduce liquid to a glaze

Adjust seasoning if necessary and remove filling from the heat

Heat the tortillas for 30seconds in a microwave

Assemble the enchiladas on a microwave safe plate and top with the remaining sauce and a sprinkle of shredded cheese (if you also ordered another recipe with cheese, you may save some for that). Microwave until cheese is melted. Serve and enjoy!
