Season chicken quarter with salt, pepper and cooking oil. Lay out on a baking sheet and roast for 10min, then reduce the heat to 400 degrees and roast for 15min.

Slice the onion, clean and chop the collards, and mince the garlic. Set aside

Dice the sweet potato. Place in a pot, cover with water and bring to a simmer.

Preheat a large saute pan over medium-high heat. Add the onion to the pan with 1/2 tablespoon of cooking oil per person and saute until vegetables begin to soften.

Add the garlic and collards to the pan, season with salt and saute until the garlic is fragrant and the collards have begun to wilt. Add 1/4cup of water per person to the pan and braise until tender.

When the potatoes are tender, remove from heat. Strain through a colander, season with salt and return to the pot. Mash the potatoes with the sour cream and adjust the seasoning as desired.

When the chicken is finished roasting, remove from the oven and set aside to rest.

Serve the roasted chicken alongside the collards and the sweet potatoes, and enjoy!
