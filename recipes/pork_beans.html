Ingredient

Equipment

Slice the onion (1/2 per person) and pepper (1/2 per person), clean and chop the escarole (1/2 head per person) set aside, mince the garlic (1 clove per person). Set aside 1/2 of the pork you recieved.

Preheat large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil and the ground pork sausage

Brown the sausage and add the onions and peppers to the pan

Allow the vegetables to soften, add the escarole to the pan and saute until it has wilted

Add the garlic and saute until fragrant

Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings here.

Add the cannellini beans and 1cup of water, bring to a simmer and reduce heat to low

Allow the mixture to stew until the pork is cooked through and the liquid has reduced by 3/4. Adjust the seasoning, serve and enjoy!
