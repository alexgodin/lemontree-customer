Season chicken thighs with bbq sauce, salt, pepper and oil.

Lay out on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25minutes

Clean and slice onion (1/2 per person), celery (1/2 stalk per person) and carrot (1 per person), dice the russet potatoes (1 per person), mince the garlic (1 clove per person) and remove the rib and roughly chop the kale (half of the provided), set aside

Place potatoes in a small pot and cover with cold water and season with salt, bring to a simmer and cook the potatoes until tender

Combine the mayo, mustard, 2 tablespoons of oil, salt and pepper in a mixing bowl, add the onion, carrot and celery and toss to combine.

Optional: add some vinegar or your favorite spices and seasonings here

When the potatoes have finished cooking, drain from the water and run cold water over the potatoes to cool down

Add the drained potatoes to the marinated vegetables, toss to combine and refrigerate until needed

Preheat a large saute pan over medium heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and the minced garlic, saute until fragrant. When garlic is fragrant, add the kale to the pan, saute until the kale begins to wilt and then add a small ammount of water to the pan and steam the kale until tender. Continue cooking until the water has evaporated

Remove the chicken from the oven when finished and adjust the seasoning on the potato salad as needed. Serve the roasted chicken with the potato salad and sauted kale, and enjoy!
