  • Bring a pot of salted water to a boil

Slice onion, mince garlic, chop kale and set aside

Cook the pasta until tender

Drain pasta and set aside

Preheat large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoon of cooking oil and the ground pork to teh pan

When the pork is browned on one side, add the onion to the pan, season with salt and saute

When the onion begins to soften, add the garlic and kale to the pan, saute until the garlic is fragrant and the kale is wilted

Add the tomato sauce to the pan, bring to a simmer and reduce by half

Add the pasta to the sauce and toss to combine

Place the sauce and noodles in the baking dish and top with the shredded mozzarella cheese

Bake for 10min and remove from the oven

Serve and enjoy!
