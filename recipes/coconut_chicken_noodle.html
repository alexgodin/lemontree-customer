Equipment

Slice the onion and carrot, chop the green beans and mince the garlice, set aside

Preheat a large saute pan over high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and the ground chicken to the pan

When browned on one side, add the onion, carrots garlic and green beans, saute until veg begins to soften and garlic becomes fragrant

Add the coconut milk and soy sauce to the pan, bring mixture to a simmer and reduce heat to low

While the sauce simmers, bring a pot of water to a boil, season with salt and cook the spaghetti until tender

When cookied, strain the pasta and add to the sauce, toss to coat and adjust the seasoning as necessary

When finished, serve the pasta and enjoy!
