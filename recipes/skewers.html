Ingredients

Equipment

Mince garlic and ginger (tip: use a spoon to peel the ginger like this video TK)

Mix garlic, ginger, soy sauce, ketchup and one tablespoon per person of vinegar together

Cut chicken thighs into one inch peices, add to soy sauce mixture and set aside

Dice onion and chop broccoli into florets (optional: cut any non woody parts of the stems into bite sized chunks)

In a medium saucepan, heat 1.5 tbsp oil per person over medium-high heat. Add the onion and cook, stirring occasionally, for 3 minutes.

Add the broccoli and cook for another 3 minutes until broccoli softens.

Add the rice and cook, stirring, for 1 minute.

Add 3/4 cup water per person and bring to a boil over high heat. Once boiling, add salt and cover, reduce the heat to low and simmer until the tender, about 15 minutes; to serve, fluff with a fork.

Core bell pepper and cut into 1 inch chunks

Thread chicken and peppers onto skewers

Preheat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add the skewers, turning, until cooked through, 8 to 10 minutes.

Plate and enjoy!
