Prep:

  • Add 2 cups water per person to a pot, bring to a boil. When it's boiling, add rice and reduce to a simmer for 20 minutes or until rice is done

Remove bottom 1" of broccoli stem. Cut broccoli head into florets and chop the stem into 1/2" pieces, set aside

Slice cubanelle pepper, set aside

Slice scallions, separating the white and green

Mince the garlic set aside

Mince the ginger, set aside

Pre-heat a large saute pan over high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and the broccoli to the pan and allow the broccoli to brown

Remove the broccoli, add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil to the pan and crumble the ground beef into the pan

Allow the beef to brown on one side, season with salt and pepper and add the sliced pepper and scallion whites.

Once pepper has softened, add the ginger and garlic

When fragrant, add the broccoli back to the pan along with the soy sauce

Adjust the seasoning to the stir fry if necessary and serve alongside the steamed rice. Top with the scallion greens and enjoy!
