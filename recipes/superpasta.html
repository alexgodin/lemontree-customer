Preheat the saute pan over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoons cooking oil and diced eggplant

Saute the eggplant until it begins to brown in the pan and season with salt, add the chopped mushrooms to the pan

When the mushroom has released it's moisture and reduced in size, add the onion and garlic to the pan

When the onion has softened, add the tomato sauce to the pan, reduce the heat to low and allow to simmer

Cook the spaghetti for 9min

Strain the pasta water and return the pasta to the large pot, add the bolognese to the pasta and continue cooking

Reduce the pasta sauce until the noodles are fully coated, add some parmesean cheese if available and enjoy!
