Equipment

Prep:

  • Bring a pot of water to a boil and season with salt

Slice the cubanelle pepper (1/2 per person), clean and chop the escarole (1/2 head per person), mince garlic (2 cloves per person). Set aside 1/2 of the pork you recieved.

Preheat a large saute pan over medium-high heat, crumble the ground pork sausace in the pan and brown the meat

Add the sliced pepper and saute until the vegetables begin to soften

Add the escarole and saute until the greens begin to wilt

Add the minced garlic and saute until fragrant

Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings here.

Add 2 cups of water to the pan, bring to a simmmer and allow the mixture to braise

Add the penne to the boiling water and cook the pasta until tender

Drain pasta and add to the pan with the sauce, continue cooking until the pasta is tender and coated in sauce and the liquid has evaporated

Serve the pasta and enjoy!
