Prep:

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees

Bring 1 cup of water per person to a boil, season water with 1 teaspoon of salt and add the rice, cover and reduce the heat to low, cook for 20min and remove from the heat

Clean and cut the onion, garlic and escarole, set aside

Slice 1/4 of the lemon into 6 half-moon slices, season the tilapia with salt, oil a baking sheet and place the fish on the sheet with 3 lemon slices over the top of each fillet. Bake the fish for 10min and remove from the oven when cooked through.

Preheat a large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and the red onion to the pan

When the onion begins to soften, add the escarole and garlic to the pan and season with salt

Saute the greens over medium heat until they have fully wilted and adjust the seasoning as necessary

Plate the sauted escarole over the rice and top with the fish fillet, serve and enjoy!
