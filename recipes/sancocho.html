Ingredient

Equipment

Slice pepper (1/2 per person) and onion (1/2 per person), Dice potatoes (1/2 lb per person) and carrots (1 per person), Slice garlic (1 clove per person)

Preheat large pot over medium heat, add 1tablespoon of oil and the chicken thighs to the pan skin side down. Season with salt and pepper

Allow the skin to render and brown, and once the fat has rendered flip the chicken thighs and add the onion and pepper to the pan

When the vegetables have softened, add the garlic and saute until fragrant

Add the potatoes and carrots to the pot, season with salt and add the tomato sauce and sazon spice packet

Allow the tomato sauce to cook down by half and then add the chicken broth and 1/2cup of water per person to the pot. Bring stew to a simmer and reduce heat to medium-low, allow to cook until potatoes and chicken are tender

When finished cooking, adjust the seasoning as needed, serve and enjoy!
