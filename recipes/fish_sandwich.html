Equipment

Prep:

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees

Cut sweet potato into wedges, season with salt and oil, lay out on a baking sheet and roast for 20min until golden and tender

Slice the red onion, set aside

Preheat large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoons of oil and the red onion, cook until onions begin to brown and add 2 tablespoons of vinegar

Reduce the vinegar until the onions are tender, sweet and lightly acidic, remove from the heat and set aside

Season the fish filets with salt and any other personal spices available, and coat the fillets on all sides with the instant grits

Preheat a large saute pan over high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and the fish fillets, cook on both sides until golden brown and crispy

Remove the fish from the pan, spread the mayonnaise on the rolls and plate the fish and onions on the rolls. Serve the sandwiches alongside the sweet potato fries and enjoy!
