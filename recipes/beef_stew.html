Dice potato (1/2 lb per person) and carrot (one carrot per person), slice the onion (1/2 onion per person) and mince the garlic (1 clove per person), set aside

Preheat pan over high heat, add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil per person and the sliced beef, sear on both sides, season with salt and remove from the pan

Add the sliced onion to the pan and saute until lightly browned

Add the carrots and garlic to the pan and saute until the garlic is fragrant

Add 1 tbsp tomato paste per person and 1/2 packet of mustard per person to the pan and saute until evenly mixed throughout the vegetables

Add the potatoes and beef broth, season lightly with salt, bring the mixture to a simmer and cook until the potatoes are tender

While the vegetables are cooking, bring 1 cup of water per person to a boil, season with salt and add the rice. Cover, reduce heat to low and cook for 20min

Once the potatoes are tender, return the seared beef to the pan and the sour cream/milk, season to taste and remove from the heat

Serve the stew over top of the rice and enjoy!
