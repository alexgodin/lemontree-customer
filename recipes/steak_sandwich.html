Ingredients

Bring 2tbsp vinegar per person, 2tbsp of water per person, 1/2 teaspoon of salt per person and a pinch of sugar per person to a boil

Slice cubanelle pepper (1/2 per person) and red onion (1/4 per person).

Add the boiled ingredients to half of the peppers and onions in a mixing bowl. Save the other half of the ingredients.

Drain the cannellini beans, rinse under cold running water, and add to the pickled vegetables, season with one tablespoon of oil per person and set aside

Remove the beef from the packaging, slice each piece in half lengthwise and season with salt, pepper, 1/2 tablespoon of oil per person and the mustard

Preheat a large saute pan over high heat, add 1 tablespoon of oil to the pan and the remaining peppers and onions

When the onion and peppers begin to brown add the marinated beef to the pan, cook for 1-2min and remove from the heat

Apply the mayonnaise to the buns, add the beef, onions and peppers, and serve alongside the white bean salad
