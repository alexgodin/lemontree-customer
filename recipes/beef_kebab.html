Equipment

Dice the onion and mince the garlic, set aside

Combine the onion, garlic and the ground beef, season with salt and pepper

Mix to combine until well blended and the vegetables are folded into the meat and roll into 2 kebabs per person

Dice the cucumber, season to taste with salt and sugar, marinate with the yogurt and set aside

Bring 1/2 cup of water per person to a boil, season with salt and add the rice, cover and reduce heat to low, cook the rice for 20min until cooked through

Preheat a large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil to the pan and brown the kebabs on all sides

Remove the kebabs from the pan and add the chopped kale, season with salt and allow the kale to cook until tender

Serve the kebabs over the rice with the sauted kale and marinated cucumbers, and enjoy!
