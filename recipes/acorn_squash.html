Slice the acorn squash, chop the carrots, dice the onion and mince the garlic, set aside

Toss the squash and carrots with oil and salt, lay out on a baking sheet and roast for 20min

Bring 1 cup of water per person to a boil, season with salt and add the rice, cover and reduce heat to low, cook for 20min

Preheat large saute pan over medium-high heat, add the sausage links to the pan and sear until golden brown on two sides

Add the onion to the pan and reduce heat to low, when the onion begins to soften add the garlic to the pan and season the veg with salt

When the garlic is fragrant, add 1/4 cup of water per person to the pan and cook until the sausages are cooked through and the vegetables are tender

Remove the sausages from the pan and add the onion and garlic to the cooked rice, mix to combine

Remove the cooked squash and carrots from the oven and set aside

Slice the sausages and plate with the squash and carrots over top of the rice, serve and enjoy!
