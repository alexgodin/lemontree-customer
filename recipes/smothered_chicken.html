Prep:

  • Add 2 cups water per person to a pot, bring to a boil.

Season chicken thighs with salt, add to a pan skin side down with 1/2 tablespoon of oil per person, turn heat to medium and allow the skin to render slowly

Slice red onion (1/2 per person), setting aside 1/4 of the onion for the salad and 3/4 of the onion for the braise. Mince the garlic (one clove per person) and dice the cucumber

When the chicken skin has browned and begun to crisp, turn over and add the onion and garlic to the pan, saute until tender

When the vegetables are tender, add the tomato paste and olives to the pan, saute until well mixed.

Add 1 cup of water per person to the pan and allow the chicken to braise for 20 minutes until the meat is tender and the sauce is reduced to a glaze

While the chicken braises, add the rice to the pot, cover and reduce heat to low, cook for 20minutes

While the chicken and rice are cooking, place the cucumber and reserved onion in a bowl, season with salt and sugar and 3/4cup of yogurt

When the chicken finishes braising, remove from the pan and add remaining yogurt to the pan. Stir to combine

Plate the chicken over the rice and top with the pan sauce, serve the marinated cucmbers on the side and enjoy!
