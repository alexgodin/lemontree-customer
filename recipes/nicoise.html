Prep:

  • Bring a pot of water to a boil.

Clean and cut green beans into 2" pieces, blanch lightly for 90 seconds and remove to a mixing bowl

Slice onion and sundried tomato, add to mixing bowl with green beans and marinate with 2 tablespoons of vinegar and salt

Add eggs to boiling water, cook for 10 minutes, remove from boiling water and run under cold water to cool

Drain tuna from its oil, lightly toss into marinating vegetables

Clean and chop leaf lettuce, add 3 tablespoons of oil to marinating vegetables, add lettuce and toss to combine

Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper if necessary and plate the salad.

Clean and slice the hard boiled eggs, place over the top of the salads and enjoy!
