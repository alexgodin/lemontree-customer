Prep:

  • Preheat oven to 450Â°

Peel squash, cut off bottom half and remove seeds

Slice squash.

Toss squash with oil, salt and pepper, lay out on a baking sheet and roast for 10 min

While the squash is cooking, remove kale from stem and chop, set aside

Slice the onion, set aside

Mince the garlic, set aside

At 10 min add the sausage links to the baking sheet with the squash and continue to cook for another 10 min

Preheat a large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and the sliced onion

Saute onion until tender, add garlic to the pan and saute until fragrant

Add the kale to the pan and 1/2 cup of water, allow the kale to steam through until it is wilted

Season the vegetables with salt and continue cooking until the water has evaporated. Optional, add your favorite spices and seasonings here.

When the squash and sausage is finished cooking, remove from the oven and slice the sausage. Plate the kale, squash and sausage, serve and enjoy!
