Prep:

  • Bring a pot of water to a boil and season with salt

Dice the onion, mince the garlic and finely chop the kale setting aside half of the kale

Combine the onion, garlic and half of the kale with the ground beef, season with salt and pepper

Mix to combine until well blended and the vegetables are folded into the meat

Form the mixture into 5-6 individual meat balls and set aside

Preheat a large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil to the pan and brown the meatballs on two sides

Add the remaining kale to the pan, saute until the greens begin to wilt and soften

Add the tomato sauce to the pan along with 1cup of water to the pan, bring the mixture to a simmer and reduce heat to medium-low

While the meatballs braise, add the pasta to the boiling water and cook until tender

Strain the pasta throuhg a colander when cooked

Add the pasta to the sauce and finish cooking the noodles with the meatballs, cooking until the sauce has reduced and glazed the noodles

Plate the pasta with the meatballs, serve and enjoy!
