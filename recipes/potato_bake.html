Prep:

  • Preheat oven to 425Â°

Slice onion, set aside

Slice carrot, set aside

Slice green beans, set aside

Remove corn kernels, set aside

Slice potatoes width wise into 1/4" coins, toss in 1 tablespoon of cooking oil and salt, set aside

Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings here.

Mince garlic, set aside

Pre-heat saute pan over high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and ground beef formed into patties, and sear on each side

Add the onion and season with salt and pepper, saute onions until they begin to caramelize. Break up the beef.

Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings here.

Add the carrots, green beans, and corn, saute lightlly and season with salt

After about 3 minutes add the minced garlic and saute until fragrant

Add 3/4 cups water per person to the pan and turn off the heat

If you have an oven safe pan you can keep the meat and vegetables in the pan and layer the potatoes over the the pan. If you do not have an oven safe pan, place the meat and vegetables in a baking dish and layer the potatoes over the dish. Bake for 20-25min or until the potatoes are tender

Adjust the seasoning if necesary, serve and enjoy!
