Dice the russet potatoes (1 potato per person) and carrots (1 carrot per person), clean and slice onion (half onion per person), remove corn kernels (1/2 ear per person), remove rib and roughly chop kale (1/2 of all kale you recieved), mince garlic (2 cloves per person)

Lay chicken, carrots and potatoes out on sheet pan. Season with salt, pepper and oil. Roast for 20-25minutes

Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings here.

Preheat a large saute pan over medium heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and the sliced onion

Saute the onion until tender, add the corn and garlic and cook until fragrant

Add the kale to the pan, saute until the kale begins to wilt and then add 1/4c of water to the pan and steam the kale until tender. Continue cooking until the water has evaporated .

Serve the roasted chicken withe the roasted and sauted vegetables and enjoy!
