Ingredients

Equipment

Prep:

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees

Season chicken thighs with salt, add to a pan with 1 tablespoon of cooking oil and turn heat to medium, allow skin/fat to render

Dice the potatoes (about 1 large potato per person)

Place in a medium pot with two peeled garlic cloves, cover with cold salted water and bring to a simmer

When the chicken skin has started to release fat and brown, remove from the pan, place on a baking sheet and roast for 20min

While the chicken and potatoes are cooking, clean and cut stringy ends off green beans, set aside

Mince the garlic, set aside

In the pan with the rendered chicken fat, add the green beans and turn the heat to medium-high

Season the beans with salt and add the minced garlic to the pan

When the garlic is fragrant, add 1/4 cup of water to the pan and allow the green beans to steam through. Reduce the water until evaporated and remove the green beans from the heat

When the potatoes are tender, strain through a colander. Reserve the cooking water in a seperate container. Transfer potatoes to a bowl (or the pot you cooked them in) and add 1/2 cup milk per person and 1/2 oz. sour cream per person.

Mash with a spoon or fork. If the potatoes are dry, add some of the reserved cooking liquid to the mash until the desired texture is achieved. Adjust the seasoning as necessary and set aside

When the chicken has finished cooking, toss the chicken and any remaining fat in the pan with the hot sauce. Serve the chicken alongside the beans and mashed potatoes, and enjoy!
