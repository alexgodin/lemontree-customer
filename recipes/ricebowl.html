Prep:

  • Preheat oven to 450Â°

Clean and slice red onion, set aside

Clean and slice red cabbage, set aside

Cut Broccoli into florets and set aside

Clean and chop cucumber, set aside

Boil 2 cups water in a small pot, add 1cup rice and 1 teaspoon salt, reduce heat and cover. Cook for 20min

In a mixing bowl, combine the onion, broccoli and cabbage, season with salt and cooking oil, lay out of a sheet tray and roast for 20min

In a mixing bowl, season the cucumbers with 1 teaspoon of salt, a small pinch of white sugar, and your favorite flavor of vinegar

When the vegetables finish roasting, combine with the marinating cucumbers and toss to combine

When the rice finishes cooking, remove from the heat, fluff with a fork and cover for an additional 10min

While the rice finishes, clean and slice the avocado and set aside

Finally, cook the egg whoever you like

When ready, plate the rice with the marinated vegetables over the top
