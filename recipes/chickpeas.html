Ingredient

  • 1/2

Equipment

In a mixing bowl, remove the ground chicken from its packaging and season with salt and any spices you might prefer

Peel and dice 1/2 onion per person to 1/4" size

Peel and dice one carrot per person to a roughly 1/4 inch size, set carrot and onion aside together

Peel and mince 2 cloves of garlic per person, setting aside

Clean your kale, remove the center rib

Roughly chop the kale, setting aside

Heat your pot over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoons of oil and add your seasoned ground chicken in four separate patties

Sear the chicken on both sides until it is nicely browned, and break up the ground meat with a rubber spatula or wooden spoon

Add your onion and carrot to the pot, seasoning with salt and saute until the vegetables begin to soften

Add your garlic to the pot and saute until fragrant

Add diced tomato and bring mixture to a simmer

Add your chopped kale, seasoning with salt and allow the greens to cook until wilted

Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings here.

Add your chickpeas

Add enough water to cover the ingredients and bring the stew to a simmer and cook over medium-low heat for 10 min

Once finished cooking, taste the stew and adjust the seasoning according to preference and enjoy!
