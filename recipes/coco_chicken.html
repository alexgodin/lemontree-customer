Equipment

Prep:

  • Add 2 cups water per person to a pot, bring to a boil. When it's boiling, add rice and reduce to a simmer for 20 minutes or until rice is done

Season ground chicken with salt and pepper, form into 4 patties, set aside

Slice half a yellow onion per person, set aside

Slice 1/2 bell pepper per person, set aside

Clean bok choy, set aside

Mince garlic, set aside

Pre-heat large saute pan over medium-high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil, add chicken patties and brown on each side

Break up the chicken patties into smaller pieces

Add sliced onion and pepper to the pan

When vegetables are tender and add bok choy and garlic

Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings here.

When garlic is fragrant, add the cocunut milk and soy sauce, bring to a simmer and allow vegetables to braise for about 5-10 minutes

When vegetables are tender and sauce has reduced, adjust seasoning and serve curry over top of the rice and enjoy!
