  • Season chicken with salt, pepper and lightly rub with cooking oil, set aside. Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings to the chicken here.

Dice potatoes and set aside

Dice carrots and set aside

Slice onion and set aside

Chop Kale and set aside

Combine vegetables in a mixing bowl and season with salt and 2 tablespoons cooking oil. Then, lay out the vegetables on a sheet tray, place the seasoned chicken legs on top of the vegetables and roast in oven for about 20 min.

While the chicken and vegetables are cooking, remove the rib from the kale, clean, lighlty chop and set aside

Clean and mince the garlic cloves, set aside

Preheat a large saute pan over medium-low heat, add garlic and 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and saute until fragrant

Add the kale to the pan, season with salt, saute until lightly wilted, add 1/4 cup of water to the pan and allow the greens to continue cooking until tender

When the water has evaporated, remove the greens from the heat, adjust the seasoning if needed and set aside

Allow chicken to rest for 5 minutes, plate the roasted chicken over top of the vegetables and enjoy!
