Equipment

Season chicken with salt, pepper and oil.

Preheat large pot over medium- high heat, add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and add the chicken to the pot. Sear the chicken thighs on each side.

While the chicken cooks, slice the onion (1/2 onion per person), celery (1/2 stalk per person) and carrot (1 per person), mince the garlic (2 cloves garlic) and de-rib and chop the kale (1/2 of the kale provided).

When the chicken is browned, add the onion, carrot and celery to the pot, saute until vegetables begin to soften

When ready, add the garlic and kale to the pan, season with salt and saute until the garlic is fragrant

Add the chicken broth to the pot in addition to 2 cups of water per person, bring to a simmer and reduce heat to medium. Allow the soup to cook for 15 min until the chicken is fully cooked

Remove the chicken, add the egg noodles and continue cooking for 5 minutes. Remove the meat from the bone and lightly shread the chicken, return to the pot and continue cooking until the noodles have finished

Adjust the seasoning as necessary and enjoy!
