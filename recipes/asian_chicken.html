Equipment

Prep:

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees

  • Boil one cup of water per person.

Season chicken thighs with salt, add to a pan with 1 tablespoon of cooking oil and turn heat to medium, allow skin/fat to render

While the chicken cooks, cut the broccoli into florets and chop the stem into bite size pieces if it is not woody and fibrous

When the chicken skin has started to release fat and brown, remove from the pan, place on a baking sheet

Season the broccoli with salt and oil, lay out on the sheet with the chicken thighs and bake for 20 min

While the chicken cooks, season water with salt and add the rice, cook for 20min and remove from the heat

Mince the garlic (1 clove per person) and ginger (one ounce per person) and slice the scallions (one per person), separate the white and green sections, set aside

In the pan with the rendered chicken fat, add the scallion whites, garlic and ginger, turn heat to medium-low and saute until fragrant

When ready, add the soy sauce, honey and 1/4 cup of water to the pan, bring to a simmer and adjust seasoning as necessary

Reduce the sauce until it is thick enough to glaze chicken, add the scallion greens and remove from the heat

When the chicken and broccoli are finished cooking, remove the chicken from the pan, spoon sauce over the chicken and set aside

Toss the broccoli in the remainder of the reserved sauce and adjust the seasoning if necessary

Serve the chicken and broccoli alongside the rice and enjoy!
