  • Preheat oven to 450Â°

  • Bring a pot of water to boil

Cut off the base of the brussels sprouts and then cut sprouts in half

Cut peppers into strips

Toss vegetables in 2 tablespoons of cooking oil and salt.

Roast in oven for 20 minutes

Clean and slice half an onion per person, set aside

Peel and mince 2 cloves of Garlic per person, set aside

Heat a pan over medium-high heat and add 2 tablespoons of oil and garlic

When garlic is fragrant, add onion to the pan

Once the onion has softened add one tablespoon of tomato paste per person

Optional: add your favorite spices and seasonings here.

after about 30 seconds add spinach to the pan

Add pasta to water and cook for 10 minutes

once spinach is wilted, add roasted vegetables and one to two cups of water and reduce to a simmer

Drain pasta and add to pot to combine. Serve and enjoy!
