Ingredients

Prep:

  • Bring two cups of water per person to boil and salt heavily

Add farro to a small pot, turn head to medium-high heat and toast the grain until fragrant, about 1min

Remove raisins from packaging, add to a mixing bowl, squeeze lemon over top and set aside

Slice red onion, add to mixing bowl with raisins and season with salt

Slice cucumber, add to mixing bowl

Clean radish bulbs and greens, cut bulbs into quarters and lightly chop the greens, add to mixing bowl (Clean extra well, Radishes can be a bit dirty.)

Slice avacado, season with salt, set aside

Drain beans in a colander and add to mixing bowl

When farro is tender (should take around 20min), taste, adjust seasoning if needed and strain through a colander

Allow the grain to cool for about 5min, then add to mixing bowl with marinating vegetables, toss to combine and adjust seasoning

Plate the salad, top with half a sliced avacado. When ready, finish the dish by frying an egg, plate on top of the salad and enjoy!
